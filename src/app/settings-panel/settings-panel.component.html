hexagonal game of life

woooh

This exists to help visualize a cs assignment a friend of mine got.

The rules are simple:

  1. alive cells survive if they have three or five alive neighbours
  2. dead cells get revived if they have two alive neighbours
  3. everything else dies or stays dead

basic controls

click any of the cells to toggle their alive state. use the buttons below to progress through the game
